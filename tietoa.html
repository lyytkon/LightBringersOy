DJ Tom Ay

Tom Ay is a well-known name for many lifestyle ravers in Finland due to his performances at underground and outdoor parties as well as festivals.

Tom Ay is a 28-year-old DJ/producer from Helsinki whom has been seen playing at various events, including the Weekend Festival in 2019, Clublifes 10th anniversary party as well as Helsinki Hardstyle Festival 2023 in which he has shared the stage with names such as Rooler, Sickmode, Rebelion, Sub Zero Project, Hard Driver and a bunch of Finnish superstars.

Spinning records since 2013. Tom Ay's style consists of extremely brutal kicks, beautiful melodies, and captivating mashups that often blend new and old sounds.

Generally when Tom Ay is in his natural habitat, the audience is commonly treated to an intense "clanging" and brutal "bass thumping" that is often known as the genre of Rawstyle/Xtra Raw. At times, his sets may also include Hardcore, Hardstyle, and even beautiful Happy Hardcore melodies.
